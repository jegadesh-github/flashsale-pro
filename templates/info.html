PRIVACY_PROTOCOL

Our policy is simple: Your data is your property. We only track what is necessary to run the terminal.

1.0 ZERO DATA RESALE

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Our revenue is generated strictly through affiliate partnerships, not through your data.

2.0 LOG DATA & COOKIES

  • Wishlist Persistence: We use local storage to keep your saved items on your device. We do not store your wishlist on our servers.
  • Performance Analytics: We use anonymized tracking to see which deals are popular so we can optimize our crawler.

3.0 NEWSLETTER SECURITY

If you join the "Inner Circle," your email is encrypted and stored in a secured SQL database. You can terminate your subscription at any time with a single click.

4.0 THIRD-PARTY TRACKING

We utilize Google Analytics and Meta Pixels strictly for **Traffic Attribution**. This means we only see *how* you found us, not *who* you are. We do not use cross-site tracking to follow your browsing habits outside of the Elite Drops Terminal.

5.0 DATA RETENTION

Inactive email data is purged from our "Inner Circle" database every 180 days. If you haven't opened an alert in 6 months, we delete your record to ensure your digital footprint stays small. **Your privacy is our uptime.**

GDPR & CCPA COMPLIANT: Users have the right to request a full data export or "Right to be Forgotten" via our concierge support.
